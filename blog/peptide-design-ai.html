January 2026 Peptide Design

AI-Driven Peptide Design at AbbVie

Therapeutic peptides sit at an exciting intersection: they can access protein–protein interfaces that small molecules often cannot, yet their design space is vast and their developability constraints are real.

At AbbVie, my work focuses on computational and AI-driven approaches to peptide design — combining generative modeling with physics-based methods to propose, filter, and optimize peptide candidates for therapeutic discovery.

Generative + physics-based

Generative models excel at exploring sequence and structure space quickly. Physics-based simulation and scoring provide the grounding — checking whether proposed peptides are stable, bind as intended, and meet developability criteria.

Looking ahead

The field is moving fast. I'll use this blog to share occasional notes on methods, benchmarks, and lessons learned at the boundary of AI and molecular design.
